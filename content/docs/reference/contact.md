If you have any questions, feedback, or need assistance with **nxthdr**, please feel free to reach out to us through the following channels:

* **Discord**: join our community on [Discord](https://discord.gg/KF3RSKXrSp) for real-time discussions, support, and collaboration with other users and developers.
* **Email**: you can also contact us via email at [contact@nxthdr.dev](mailto:contact@nxthdr.dev).
* **GitHub**: for technical issues, feature requests, or contributions to a given project, please open an issue or pull request on the GitHub repository of that project under the **nxthdr** [organization](https://github.com/nxthdr).

## Reporting Abuse

If you encounter or suspect abuse of our services (PeerLab or Saimiris), please report it immediately to [abuse@nxthdr.dev](mailto:abuse@nxthdr.dev).

When reporting abuse, please include (if possible):

* The service involved (PeerLab or Saimiris)
* IP address or prefix involved
* Date and time of the incident
* Description of the abusive behavior
* Any supporting evidence (logs, screenshots, etc.)

We take all abuse reports seriously and will investigate promptly. All reports are handled confidentially.
